History of Our Furniture Company

Our business has come a long way and continues to grow.

1995 — Our company was founded by John Smith in a small workshop.
1998 — We moved to a larger facility to accommodate our growing team and production capabilities.
2001 — Our company expanded its product line to include custom-made furniture pieces for corporate clients.
2005 — We opened our first retail showroom in London to offer our furniture directly to consumers.
2010 — Our company implemented environmentally friendly practices, including the use of sustainable materials and energy-efficient machinery.
2023 — We continue to provide high-quality furniture to our clients and remain committed to sustainable and ethical manufacturing practices.
